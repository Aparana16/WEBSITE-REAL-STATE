Frequently Asked Questions:-

What is the Best price Guarantee?

When booking through our website www.ApnaGhar.com, via our service center or directly in our hotel, we guarantee, that you will get the best available rate.

What is the daily rate?

The so-called daily rate is the most flexible and best available rate without any booking restrictions with the exception of fair dates or other high season dates.

How can I order rooms flyers/brochures?

If you wish to read our hotel information at home in a printed version, you can order our hotel flyer and brochures online with the brochure request form or you can contact our service center under +1234567890 or website www.ApnaGhar.com

Do you offer handicapped rooms?

Yes, some Hotels provide handicapped rooms with appropriate accessible equipment. If your requested hotel offers the rooms.

Can I reserve a parking lot?

In some hotels you can book a parking lot directly in your online booking. After choosing a hotel during the booking process, the system offers you some additional services. Please note that you have to choose a service for each day. It is not possible to reserve a parking slot in advance

What are the booking opening hours?

The booking and website will open 24*7 hours and our colleagues will be at your service day and night.

Can I book tee times online?

Unfortunately this is not possible online, but you can contact the house directly, which will be happy to reserve a tee time for you.

Can I bring my pet?

In almost every ApnaGhar Hotel & Resort pets are welcome. Depending on the hotel, there is a fee for pets. Please note that for hygienic reasons pets are usually not allowed in the gastronomic and spa areas.

Are the rates per person or per room?

All prices, unless stated otherwise, are on a per-room-basis.

Can I book two rooms of different categories?

At the moment this is not possible online, without making two separate bookings. If you require two different categories, you can contact our houses directly.

Can I book two rooms with different occupancy?

No, unfortunately this is not possible at the moment. You will have to make two separate bookings or contact the home directly

Which size are the rooms?

The sizes of the rooms vary from hotel to hotel. Also within a hotel they vary between each category. Customers can select the room size that how much big room the user requires.

Do you offer non-smoking rooms?

Yes we offer non-smoking rooms. In some hotels every room is non-smoking.

Do you offer a child rate?

Yes, ApnaGhar Houses & Resorts offer family-friendly rates. Children from 0-11 years stay free. Children from 12-16 years pay, depending on the hotel. There are also discounts on meals: Children from 0-5 years receive 100%, children from 6-11 years 50% and children from 12-16 years 25% discount on price.

What should I do, if I lost or forgot something in my room?

Please contact the house directly. Our rooms will be cleaned daily, so that your item probably has been found by our housekeeping team.

How do I know if the hotel is bookable?

Please choose the hotel you would like to book in the booking search box on the upper left side and after that you can receive a message or mail that your room has been booked. Where the dates are marked as bookable (green) or not bookable (red).

How can I see the different rates?

As soon as you have chosen a hotel and have entered all travel dates, you will see our rate grid, which will show the lowest available rates for each category. Then the option to display all available rates by clicking on a room category.

How many rooms can be booked at once?

For each reservation you can book up to 3 rooms online. If you require more rooms, please contact our service center or the hotel.

How can I see which features are included in the price?

A detailed overview will be shown beneath the rate grid, if you click on a rate or category. The rate description will show every included service.

Can I book without a credit card?

No, for booking room you need a card for booking or you can also use online payment like: PayTm, Paypal, google pay etc.
